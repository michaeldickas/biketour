Filling up the tank

Posted on: 15/05/2015 /
Categories: Travel

Posted from Figueres, Catalonia, Spain.

This morning I didn´t feel for exercise and decided to have another day of rest.

After the breakfast I was looking for a physio and managed to get a massage of my legs at noon. It was one of these “what have I done to this lady?”-massages, I almost was crying out loud of pain, but now it feels like heaven. Then I washed some clothes, fixed some issues with the bike, had a huge and delicious hamburger for lunch and now I enjoy the day.

This is the good side of travelling on your own. You can eat when and what you want, sleep when you want, you can simply do whatever you want and don´t have to ask or discuss with anybody.

The weather forecast was bad in that there should be strong winds from the north today. The morning, however, was quite calm and the wind started only after noon. The wind is very gusty and cycling would be very difficult and even dangerous. I will get up early tomorrow and try to get as far as possible before the wind comes up.
