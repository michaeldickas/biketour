Let it flow

Posted on: 12/05/2015 /
Categories: Travel

Posted from Calella, Catalonia, Spain.

I had a good day! I simply let it flow and the therapy works. Slow travel is good travel.

The centre of Barcelona was great as usual. The rest of the route was not very nice, it went through large industrial zones and heavy traffic. But I had a good lunch on the beach in Arenys del Mar.

The hotel is good and the bar keeper looks like a nice guy. He speaks some german and I will see the football match with him.

Todays route: 81,85 km
