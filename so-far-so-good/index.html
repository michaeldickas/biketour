So far, so good!

Posted on: 06/05/2015 /
Categories: Travel

Posted from Castellón de la Plana, Valencian Community, Spain.

Last night I was really worried about my knee, I hardly could enjoy watching the CL football match. I cooled the knee the whole evening with ice cubes, but the pain didn´t go away and it still hurt this morning. At the breakfast I was really down, I thought I had to cancel the tour already on the morning of the third day!  I cooled the knee with ice again and finally gave it a try and started to Valencia, which was 20 km away. Luckily, it went well and in the first pharmacy I bought some medicine.  10 minutes later all the pain was gone (fortunately I don´t have to undergo any doping controls). You can imagine what relief I felt.

I was really happy because I could solve in the first two days two unforeseen, but really mission critical issues: the deficient navigation system and my knee pain. With this great feeling I really enjoyed the day and everything went easy.  The route went through endless orange plantations in parallel to the highway A7.  The profile was totally flat, the bridges over the highway were probably the highest elevations. And in some parts I had even tailwind!

I am now in Castellon and was looking forward to have a pleasant and relaxed CL football evening, but Messi spoiled it completely. So only an almost perfect day!

Todays route: 104,33 km
