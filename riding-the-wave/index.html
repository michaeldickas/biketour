Riding the wave

Posted on: 02/06/2015 /
Categories: Travel

Posted from Neu-Ulm, Bavaria, Germany.

A very long stage, because I had good tail winds and a steady downhill again. I am riding on a wave of good energy coming from SW.

The best came at the end of the day. Just opposite the hotel is a Thai Massage. I got another one of these traditional power massages I had enjoyed already in Lyon. Now I feel tired but relaxed.

After the massage I will give the body a day of rest. Ulm looks nice and I will stay here tomorrow.
