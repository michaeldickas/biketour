On the home stretch

Posted on: 05/06/2015 /
Categories: Travel

Posted from Gunzenhausen, Bavaria, Germany.

The hottest day of the year in Germany, I was grilling on the bike. I have left the Danube valley and the terrain has turned quite hilly again. But so closed to the end of the tour the happiness hormones are at sky high level and nothing can stop me.

With the bank holiday, school holidays and the summer weather it is a high season weekend here and everything is booked out. I had planned to spend the last night in a good hotel, but the only accomodation I could get is a small room in a private house. But you know, with the happiness hormones all is peace and harmony …

I am in the franconian lakes region and it is really hot and I will go for a swim now.

Today´s route: 70,92 km
