In good company

Posted on: 27/05/2015 /
Categories: Travel

Posted from Besançon, Franche-Comté, France.

I made two great discoveries today.

On the first kilometers this morning I met Markus on my way. He is a german guy who is on a bike tour from Nantes to Freiburg. We went together the whole way to Besançon. Both of us were talking a lot, so time was flying.

It was not totally by chance that I met him. Already the last kilometers yesterday and the whole route today I was cycling on an european bike path which goes from the Atlantic to the Black Sea. There are many bikers now in May, most of them germans.

The route follows the course of rivers and thus it is generally quite flat making it ideal cycling territory. It is very well signalled, so you don´t even need a navi or map and there are no cars nor trucks. The only thing we missed were some bars for a rest. I will definitely follow the route on the next days until Mulhouse, maybe even longer.

For tomorrow things look great as well. I will cycle with Markus again and the forecast for the next two days says that there will be winds from West, unbelievable!

Today´s route: 63,91 km
