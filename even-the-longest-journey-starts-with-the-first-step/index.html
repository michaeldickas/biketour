Even the longest journey starts with the first step …

Posted on: 04/05/2015 /
Categories: Travel

Posted from Moraira, Valencian Community, Spain.

I am lying on my hotel bed in Moraira and feel exhausted, but happy. I finally made it, but it was a hard one …

The day started very good with ideal weather conditions. I made good progress until my navigation system started playing dirty tricks with me. Near Villajoyosa it sent me on small dusty and stony paths where you you would have had problems even with a mountain bike. I finally made it until Altea and was enjoying to ride on a nice bike lane directly at the seafront when all of a sudden it ended and I stood in the middle of nowhere on a lonely pebble beach. And at the end of today´s trip the best: instead of guiding me on the flat paths between Calpe and Moraira it sent me up a mountain (“cumbre gran sol”) for nothing. I had to go up 300m just to go down again! I definitely have to work on the route criteria!

The good news is that my body does not complain, nothing hurts, not even the buttocks.

Thanks a lot for all the comments, this is really encouraging!

Todays route: 90,28 km
